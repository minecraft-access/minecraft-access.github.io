Minecraft Access

A Minecraft mod for Fabric/Neoforge that helps blind and low vision players play the game.

Download Documentation

What is it?

Minecraft Access enhances accessibility for blind and low vision players by using speech and sound cues to provide crucial in-game information. It enables blind and low vision players to experience the game in a way that was previously impossible.

Features

Minecraft Access has a lot of features that enhance accessibility. Some of them include:

  • Read blocks you're facing
  • Sound cues for locating points of interests like mobs and ores
  • Full menu narration, including of all in-game screens
  • Much, much more
  • See the full feature list

How to install?

Installing Minecraft Access is as easy as installing any other mod:

  • Install Fabric or Neoforge
  • Download Minecraft Access
  • Place it in the mods folder
  • Launch the game and enjoy
